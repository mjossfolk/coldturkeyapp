An easy way to quit smoking. Track your progress and financial savings, see real time health benfits and manage cravings.

Understand How Your
Health Changes

Live updates on how your health is effected over time when you quit smoking. This helps you see the benfits of quitting every step of the way.

Reward yourself with your savings

  • Real time updates on how much you have saved from quitting

  • Create goals like a down payment for a car or a new pair of shoes

  • Allocate savings to each goal and see how long before you can afford it

Track Cravings, including Locations.

Track patterns in your smoking habits by recording where you have cravings, the intensity and your mood at the time. This can help you avoid places and situations which make you want to smoke again.
